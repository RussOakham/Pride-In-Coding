Pride In Coding

Let's celebrate pride together! Come code with us

What we do:

Pride in Coding is a one day learning experience, designed to empower the LGBTQ+ and trans community within the tech industry. Run by world class instructors, our one day online camp is the perfect opportunity for students to get their first taste of software development.

Learning how to code is more than education — It’s economic and social opportunity and our community is determined to provide these skills to as many LGBTQ+ and trans individuals as possible.

About the day:

You will Learn the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. We believe that you learn best by doing, so throughout the day you will work with our instructors, as well as your fellow students on a number of coding challenges.

You'll learn the basics of why user experience (UX) is important, the design basics and the tools used to define them.

By the end of the day, you will have built, styled and programmed your first-ever interactive webpage.
